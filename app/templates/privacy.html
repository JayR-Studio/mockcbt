PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

MockCBT respects your privacy. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect user information when you use our exam preparation platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, login details, activation status, exam attempts, scores, and answer history.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to create your account, activate your access, save your exam attempts, display results, improve the platform, and provide support.

Exam Data

Your scores and answers are stored so you can review past attempts and track your progress. We do not sell your personal exam data.

Payments and Activation

If payment is handled manually, proof of payment may be reviewed only for the purpose of confirming access.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security. Users are advised to keep their login details private.

Independence Disclaimer

MockCBT is an independent educational preparation platform. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Civil Service Commission, POLAC, the Nigeria Police Force, or any government institution.

Contact

For privacy questions or account support, please contact us through the contact page.
